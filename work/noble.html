Noble


2020

Graphic Design

Artwork Design

Video Editing

Noble is the artistic name of Pedro Fidalgo, a 22-year-old musician from Amarante, Portugal. Noble has topped the Portuguese album and radio airplay charts for over 3 months with “Honey”, his first single and album, which included a car concert tour in the midst of the global pandemic.

To thank and broaden the audience that brought Noble to the top of the charts, a couple communication pieces were made using a new graphic identity from the artist's logo - dubbed "the pill", which represented each of the achievements with the first album.

In December, Noble partnered with Ronald McDonald House Charities Portugal (RMHC) to make a song celebrating Christmas - "It's Christmas Everywhere" - featuring fellow Portuguese singer-songwriters SYRO, Zé Manel, Meestre and Gabriela Couto. As well as a special t-shirt designed by Júlio Torcato, all proceeds wentto support the RMHC's Christmas 2020 efforts.
