Turbina

2020

Service: Branding Identity, Product Design

Turbina is an arts producer and publisher based in the heart of Porto, established in 2006 by Pedro Nascimento. The curator provides a platform that aims at the implementation of cultural projects in the various means of artistic creation, using minimalist resources and alternative languages.

In the face of an ecological concern and the levelling of visual communication, the graphical stamp is reduced to its minimum. The aim is to reduce visual noise, both physically and digitally, always within the framework of the promotion of culture and the artistic aptitude of the artists representing Turbina, which belongs to a large cultural and artistic ecosystem network.
