That Pupi


2021

Branding Identity

That Pupi is a graphic designer based in Santiago, Chile, popular for her work with Y2K Aesthetic Institute and Chilean singer-songwriter Namuel.

As a tribute to Namco's MR. DRILLER game franchise, I created an icon and wordmark for That Pupi that fits in the Y2K aesthetic whilst being modern.
