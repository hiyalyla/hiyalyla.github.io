Arenataping

2018

Service: Branding Identity, Graphic Design

Arenataping is a pro-wrestling music community project based in Europe, best described as the No. 1 source for pro-wrestling music news and insight. The project has reached mainstream media with reports appearing on WrestleTalk, Ringside News and Heel By Nature.

The main visual element of the Arenataping brand is the "echo", which symbolises the reverb heard in arenas on pro-wrestling events as the music plays through the speakers, bringing emotions of all shapes and sizes to the audience.
