Apstineo Force Polonia

2020

Service: Graphic Design, Merchandise Design

Apstineo Force Polonia (AFP) is a Polish simracing team founded in 2019, that is rapidly rising up the ranks. Since early 2020, it has been competing exclusively on the rFactor 2 platform.

The eagle is the icon of the team, representing the eagerness and determination for success. Basing from the icon, the visual communication of the team have to e qually resemble the same values that push it forward.
