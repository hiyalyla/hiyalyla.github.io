Grmelli Racing


2020

Branding Identity

Livery Design

Grmelli Racing is an American simracing team estabilished in 2015. After finding success in open-wheel single-seater leagues, the team branched out to endurance racing, having competed in leagues like SFR, VMG and VISC.

To celebrate its 5 years since estabilishment, a rebrand was made in retro fashion, inspired by the design language of the mid-90s. A modified typeface was made for the occasion.
