Meestre

2020

Service: Logotype, Artwork Design, Video Editing

Meestre is a 28-year old singer-songwriter and trumpeter from Seixal, Portugal. Whilst being a music teacher, he got singing gigs with Ricardo Carriço and world renowned singer-songwriter Mika. Meestre's sound mixes the soul of pop and the musical intensity immortal by time and cherished by dreams.

After participating in two The Voice programs during 2015 and 2017, Meestre kickstarted his solo career in 2020 with “YOU”, a love ballad that connects and fixes hearts in a new reality midst a pandemic. I was assigned to do the artwork and lyric music video of the single, and a logotype for the artist.
