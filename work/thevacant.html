The Vacant


2017

Logotype

The Vacant is a five-piece alternative rock group hailing from Bath, UK. The band's sound is described by its power and texture infused with elements of electronic, shoegaze and indie, inviting comparisons to the likes of Klangstof, Slowdive and Zola Blood. The band prides itself on writing and producing all of its music and content from their home studio.

A band needs its own visual identity that welcomes the listener to their offering experience and The Vacant was no exception. I made a custom typeface that best described the band at the surface - levelled, welcoming, but quite mysterious.
