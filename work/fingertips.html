Fingertips

2020

Service: Branding Identity, Graphic Design

Fingertips is a pop-rock band from Viseu, Portugal, formed in 2002. The band has overcome grand national success with No. 1 sales and radio play positions across 10 years.

As the band prepares its new record, I did a visual update of the Fingertips wordmark and graphical elements that describe the band's legacy in the Portuguese pop music culture of the noughties.
