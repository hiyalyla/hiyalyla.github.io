Williams Racing is a British Formula 1 team formed in 1977 by Frank Williams and Patrick Head, having won 16 FIA Formula One World Championship titles in the past 40 years. The team exists purely to race in the top echelon of motor racing, with the Williams name becoming synonymous with top-level motorsport since the 1960s.

The project's objective is to carry out a reformulation of the Williams Racing brand that connects its history with the vanguardism of its medium in a succinct and clean communication.

The logo is made up of two elements - the icon and the wordmark. The icon is based on the first Williams logo, the initial letter W to join the number 1, which represents the avant-garde of the team, in a futuristic and minimalist style, with an angle that reproduces the feeling of dynamism and speed present in it.

The palette was chosen not only because it represents the team's historical past, but also for its serenity, harmony, vivacity and energy - adjectives that fit perfectly into the brand.
