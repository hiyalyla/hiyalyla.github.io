World Wrestling Entertainment, Inc (WWE) is an American entertainment company that is primarily known for pro-wrestling, consisting of a portfolio of businesses that create and deliver original content 52 weeks a year to a global audience, reaching more than 800 million homes worldwide in 28 languages.

To celebrate WWE superstar Keith Lee's call-up to the main roster, I made a set of artwork pieces in the style of the company's record label's music cover artwork, as Keith received a new entrance music theme titled "First of My Kind". Keith Lee posted the artwork on his social media on Veteran's Day.
